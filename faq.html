Frequently asked questions

You can play Fortnite on PC, Mac, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, and Android and iOS devices.
To play Fortnite, you need to create an account and download the game. Once you have downloaded the game,
you can sign in and start playing.

Fortnite has a variety of game modes, including Battle Royale, Save the World, and Creative. Battle Royale is a free-to-play mode where you fight to be the last one standing. Save the World is a cooperative mode where you and your friends fight against zombies. Creative is a mode where you can build and create your own worlds.

You can build forts in Fortnite by using materials that you find in the game world. To build a fort, you need to select the materials you want to use and then place them in the world. You can also use traps and weapons to defend your fort.

You can get skins in Fortnite by purchasing them from the in-game store or by completing challenges. Skins are cosmetic items that change the appearance of your character. They do not affect the gameplay in any way.

If you find a bug in Fortnite, you can report it by going to the game's website and submitting a ticket. You can also report bugs by tweeting at the Fortnite developers.
